The time I studied for an English test.


It was 1:00 am when I realized that there was a test the next day for school. Specifically English, my weakest subject. I sighed and rolled out of bed. I grabbed my phone and walked downstairs.

I logged into my computer and opened up Chrome. I searched for Shakespeare Act II study guide and started reading through the material. I started falling asleep, so I asked:
